Quick way of finding the \(n\)-th fibonacci number with a time complexity of \(O(\log n)\). $$\left( \matrix{ 1 & 1 \cr 1 & 0 \cr} \right)^n = \left( \matrix{ F_{n+1} & F_n \cr F_n & F_{n-1} \cr} \right)$$ $$\left( \matrix{ 1 & 1 \cr 1 & 0 \cr} \right)^n \left( \matrix{ 1 \cr 0 \cr} \right) = \left( \matrix{ F_{n+1} \cr F_n \cr} \right)$$
